Odin's Ayam Bakar

Back to home page

Description

Ayam Bakar coupled with rice after completion. This ayam bakar, or Indonesian grilled chicken, is a simple version with easy-to-find ingredients. Chicken thighs simmer in an incredibly fragrant sauce, and then are finished by charring on a charcoal grill. You can serve this chicken with freshly sliced or diced red chilies, freshly chopped cilantro, and a few lime wedges alongside.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sliced shallots
  • Six cloves of garlic, peeled
  • 1 (3 inch) piece ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, inside white part only, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon sambal chili sauce, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 leaf bay leaves
  • 6 thigh with skin chicken thighs
